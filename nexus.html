✨ Our Story

Redefining Campus Innovation Since 2019

NEXUS began with a simple belief: every student deserves a platform to showcase their potential. What started as a small college hackathon has grown into India's most anticipated inter-college technology festival.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry readiness, empowering the next generation of innovators with hands-on experience, mentorship, and real-world exposure.

❓ FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about NEXUS 2026. Can't find your answer? Reach out to us.

Any student currently enrolled in an undergraduate or postgraduate program at a recognized college or university in India can participate. There are no branch or year restrictions. You can register individually or as a team depending on the event category.
Early bird registration is completely free until July 1, 2026. After that, a nominal fee of ₹199 per participant applies. Team registrations (up to 4 members) get a 20% discount on the total fee.
Yes! All registered participants from outstation will receive free accommodation in campus hostels. Meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner, and midnight snacks during hackathon) are provided at no extra charge for all participants.
Absolutely! You can register for up to 3 events as long as their schedules don't overlap. The detailed schedule will be shared with shortlisted participants via email. We recommend focusing on 1–2 events for the best experience.
Bring your laptop, charger, student ID, and a lot of enthusiasm! For robotics events, you can bring your own components or use the ones we provide. Wi-Fi, power strips, and working spaces are all available at the venue.
Each event has its own judging criteria and panel of industry expert judges. Generally, projects are evaluated on innovation, technical complexity, real-world impact, presentation quality, and code quality. Detailed rubrics are shared before each event.
